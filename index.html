About Us

The Information Security Club is a club dedicated to educating students about various aspects of offensive and defensive information security. To give back to the community, some community projects that the club participates in include contributing code to make free and open source software even better, and contributing writeups/tutorials online to assist beginning information security students.


We meet every Tuesday at lunch in room 513! Come to our meetings!
